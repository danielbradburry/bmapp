Buying Or Building A New Home

Why use a Real Estate agent to buy a New Home?

A New Home sales person works for the builder and is responsible for getting the best terms and price for the builder. When you have a real estate agent/buyer’s agent, they legally have a fiduciary duty to get the most favorable terms, and look out for your best interests.

“A fiduciary is a person who has a high duty of care for another person, the client. The law requires the fiduciary to place the client’s interest ahead of his or her interest.”

Real estate agents are overseen by the Real Estate Commission, are professionals and are there to advise you. If you do not have one with you, you might be at a disadvantage in contracts and negotiating with sellers and builders. If you intend to hire a buyer’s agent, you must do so before walking into the property the first time. Make sure you take your buyer’s agent with you.
